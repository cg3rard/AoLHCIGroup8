About LimbahKu

Turning waste into opportunity and empowering communities to create a cleaner future.

Our Mission

At LimbahKu, we believe waste is not the end of a product’s life — it is the beginning of a new opportunity.

Our platform connects individuals, recyclers, and collectors so that recyclable materials can be traded easily and responsibly.

By empowering communities to manage waste efficiently, we aim to reduce environmental impact while creating economic opportunities.

Why LimbahKu?

Many recyclable materials end up in landfills simply because there is no easy way to connect people who have waste with those who need it.

LimbahKu bridges that gap by providing a digital marketplace for waste materials, helping both buyers and sellers benefit from sustainable practices.

Together, we can turn waste into value and build a cleaner, greener future.
